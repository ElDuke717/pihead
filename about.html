What is this?

This is a blog and weather app hosted on a Raspberry Pi 3B. It pulls data from the Open Weather API for this area of Central Virginia and will eventually have real time local weather data provided by a Sense Hat and weather station.

This is primarily a way for my family and I to measure weather around our home in real time and experiment with the Raspberry Pi.
